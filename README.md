## About Me

I'm a data analyst with a background in **Business Intelligence & Big Data Analytics**, passionate about extracting meaningful patterns from complex datasets and translating them into clear, actionable insights.

My work spans the full analytics lifecycle, from raw data ingestion and SQL-based cleaning to exploratory analysis, statistical modeling, and interactive dashboards. I care about the *story behind the numbers* just as much as the numbers themselves.

I've worked with datasets across **healthcare, retail, energy, and entertainment**, giving me a broad perspective on how data challenges differ by domain, and how to adapt accordingly. Whether it's optimizing a SQL query, designing a Power BI dashboard, or building a churn prediction model, I focus on work that creates real business value.
